Hi everyone,


Long time no talk!

Bria Reborn has been dormant for quite some time. If you stopped by at any point in the last few years, you may have been greeted by a cryptic page or even a new forum. We were working on a comeback a few years back, but sadly, we just didn't have the time to put in the effort that the community deserved. At the very least, we have reclaimed our domain name from domain squatters. We decided to keep this page up as a memorial to our community.

We hope that you remember Bria Reborn with fondness. Many of us made friends and forged bonds that still last today. Bria Reborn was founded by people who had a burning passion for Star Wars Galaxies. And sure, sometimes that passion brought drama, but we persevered and made it work.

Star Wars Galaxies, SWGEmu, Bria and Bria Reborn will always have a special place in our hearts. We wish you all the best in whatever or wherever life brings you next.


May the force be with you... always,
